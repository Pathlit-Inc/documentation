## Overview
The LinkedIn Output Node is a feature in the Pathlit workflow builder that enables users to post content directly to their LinkedIn profile. This node connects to your LinkedIn account and allows you to share updates, articles, or any relevant content with your connections or the public. You can choose to limit your posts to just your connections if desired.

## Configuration Parameters

To set up the LinkedIn Output Node, you need to sign in to your LinkedIn account and configure the following parameters:

* **Post Content**:

    The content you wish to share on LinkedIn.

    Example value: `8 Reasons I love Pathlit for building AI workflows to automate my tasks...`

* **Post to Connections**:

    A checkbox option that allows you to choose whether to share the post with your connections only or to the public.

## Expected Inputs and Outputs
- **Inputs**:

  This node can take any input from previous nodes in the workflow, such as content generated by LLMs or data retrieved from other sources. Use the `{node-name output-name}` syntax to reference the data you want to post from the input connections.

- **Outputs**:

  The output will indicate the status of the LinkedIn post, confirming whether it was successful or if any errors occurred.

## Use Case Examples
1. **Scheduled Company Updates Sharing**:

    If you want to keep your professional network informed about new product launches or company milestones, you can use the LinkedIn Output Node to automatically post these updates from your workflow.

2. **Engaging with Your Audience**:

    For marketers, this node can be used to share engaging content or articles that resonate with your audience, driving traffic and interaction on your LinkedIn profile. You can use LLMs to generate content that is tailored to your target audience.

## Error Handling and Troubleshooting
- **Network Problems**:

    If there are connectivity issues, you may experience delays or failures in posting. Please check your internet connection and ensure LinkedIn services are operational.

If you face any challenges with the LinkedIn Output Node that are not addressed in this documentation, please reach out to our [support team](mailto:dev@pathlit.ai) for assistance.
