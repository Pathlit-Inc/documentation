---
title: 'Email'
description: 'Exports the output via Email to single or multiple users'
---

### Fields in Email node

- **Recipients:** Recipients of the email, must be a comma separated list of valid email addresses without spaces
- **Subject:** Subject of the email
- **Body:** Connect the node to an LLM, for example, to use the result of the LLM as content for the email. The content includes the signature.
